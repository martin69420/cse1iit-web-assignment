Campus

Latrobe's Bundoora campus is one of a kind. At a size of 235 hectares, the campus resembles its own small town. There are a wide array of different facilities available, such as restaurants, shops, countless places to work and study, and venues for events and functions. See Facilities for more details.
The campus also has a large parking area, and good connections to public transport. See Parking and Travel for more details.
